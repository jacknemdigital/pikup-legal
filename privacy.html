Pikup

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Pikup ("we," "our," or "us") is a social sports coordination app that helps people find others to play sports with at public courts and parks. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name, username (handle), and profile photo
  • City and optional bio
  • Sports preferences and skill level
  • Apple ID (used for Sign in with Apple authentication)

Information generated by your activity:

  • Check-ins at courts and parks (when your account is set to Active)
  • Sessions you create or join
  • Messages sent in session group chats
  • Courts you add to the platform
  • Friends and connections

Device and technical information:

  • Location data (only when you use location-based features such as finding nearby courts or checking in)
  • Device type, operating system version
  • Push notification token (if you enable notifications)
  • Crash logs and performance data for debugging

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your profile and activity to other users
  • To show nearby courts and check-ins on the map
  • To match you with sessions and players near you
  • To send push notifications about session activity (if you opt in)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs
  • To prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Privacy Controls

You have meaningful control over your visibility on Pikup:

  • Active / Inactive toggle: When set to Inactive, your check-ins will not appear in the community feed or on other users' maps. You can browse and view content normally.
  • Location access: You can deny or revoke location permissions at any time in your iOS Settings. Core features like the map will still work with reduced functionality.
  • Notifications: You can opt out of push notifications at any time in Settings → Notifications → Pikup.
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app or by contacting us at the email below.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Other Pikup users: Your profile, sports, skill level, and activity (when Active) are visible to other users on the platform.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and cloud storage. We use RevenueCat for subscription management. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer. Community-generated content (such as courts you added) may remain on the platform in anonymized form.

6. Children's Privacy

Pikup is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Security Rules, encrypted data transmission (TLS), and Sign in with Apple to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Services

Court data is sourced from OpenStreetMap, which is publicly available under the Open Database License. Map tiles and location features use Apple Maps (MapKit), subject to Apple's privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Pikup after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
